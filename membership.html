Membership description:
Becoming a member of the Cridersville Historical Society is a meaningful way to help
preserve our local heritage for future generations. Through museum exhibits, public
education programs, and activities with local students, our members support the work of
saving and sharing Cridersville’s history. Membership is open to individuals, students,
families, patrons, and businesses, with affordable annual options available. It is important
work—and it is fun. We invite you to join us and be part of preserving our community’s
story.
