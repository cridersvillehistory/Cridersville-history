Membership

Becoming a member of the Cridersville Historical Society is a meaningful way to help preserve our local heritage for future generations. Through museum exhibits, public education programs, and activities with local students, our members support the work of saving and sharing Cridersville’s history. Membership is open to individuals, students, families, patrons, and businesses, with affordable annual options available. It is important work and it is fun. We invite you to join us and be part of preserving our community’s story.

Membership Levels

Membership Type Annual Cost Individual $10 Student $5 Dual (Couple) $20 Patron $50, $100, or $250 Corporate $100 or $250
