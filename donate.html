The Historical Society is a 501(c) (3) charitable organization.
Donation use statement:
Unless a donation is specifically designated for a particular program or initiative, all
contributions to the Cridersville Historical Society are placed into the Society’s general fund.
The Board of Trustees reviews the needs of the organization and determines how donated
funds can be used most effectively to support preservation efforts, facility maintenance and
expansion, educational programming, and community events.
