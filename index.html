Preserving Our Past For Future Generations Welcome paragraph: Welcome to the Cridersville Historical Society, where local history comes to life through community, tradition, and shared experiences. Our Local History Museum is open to the public on the second and fourth Sundays from April through October, and throughout the year we host a variety of special events, open houses, and fundraisers that bring people together while celebrating Cridersville’s past. From school tours and youth programs to seasonal events at the Bowsher Log Home and museum, we invite you to explore, learn, and enjoy the stories that make our community unique.
