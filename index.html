Welcome!

The Cridersville Historical Society preserves Cridersville’s heritage through museum exhibits, educational programs, community events, and stewardship of historic places.

Established in 1987 when the Bowsher Family donated the restored Daniel and Catherine Bowsher Log Home, the Society later acquired the former EUB Church on W. Sugar Street to expand space for artifacts, photographs, and documents.
