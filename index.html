Welcome

Welcome to the Cridersville Historical Society, where local history comes to life through community, tradition, and shared experiences. Our Local History Museum is open to the public on the second and fourth Sundays from April through October, and throughout the year we host a variety of special events, open houses, and fundraisers that bring people together while celebrating Cridersville’s past.
